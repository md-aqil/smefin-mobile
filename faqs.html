Frequently Asked Questions

We have put together a list of commonly asked question below. if the answer to your question is not found on this page, then please contact our customer support team.

  • Who can apply for funding?

    FinCompare provides a variety of instruments for industry-independent corporate financing. Corporations and partnerships, the self-employed and freelancers with operational activities can apply for financing.

    As a rule, a trading history of min. 2 years necessary. In exceptional cases (e.g. in the case of strong growth), financing is also possible for younger companies with selected partners.

    The financing can be used flexibly - for example to make an investment, to sell receivables and generate liquidity in the process, to buy goods or to refinance existing liabilities more cheaply .
